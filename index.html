Part A — Analytical Solutions (No Calculator)  [24 pts]
  1. Find the general solution of the differential equation:   dy/dx = 3x2 + 4x − 5
  2. Using L'Hôpital's Rule, evaluate:   limx→∞ (2x + 5)/(3x − 2)
  3. Find all local extrema of  f(x) = x3 − 3x + 2. Classify each as a local max or local min using the Second Derivative Test.

  3. Sketch the direction field for y′ = y − x over [−2, 2] × [−2, 2] and draw the solution curve through (0, 1). (10 pts)
